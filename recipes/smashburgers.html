Smash Burgers

Description

This smash burger recipe makes super juicy burgers with crispy edges. Use a grill for faster cooktime.

Ingredients

  • 2-8 buns
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • Salt
  • American cheese
  • Toppings of choice
  • Parchment paper

Steps

  1. Prepare and heat the griddle.
  2. Brown the buns.
  3. Form the meat into balls.
  4. Place each ball on the grill, cover with parchmant paper, and smash.
  5. Sprinkle patties with salt.
  6. Take off grill and put into buns with desired toppings.
