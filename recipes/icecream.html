Ice Cream

Description

This homemade ice cream is easy to make with just 4 ingredients: pure vanilla extract, milk, heavy whipping cream, and sugar. This American or Philadelphia-style ice cream makes for a brighter, more pronounced vanilla flavor.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Heavy whipping cream
  • Cane Sugar
  • Vanilla or desired flavoring

Steps

  1. Cook the milk, cream, and sugar until the sugar has dissolved.
  2. Transfer the mixture to a measuring cup, then add flavoring. Chill overnight
  3. Pour mixture into ice cream maker and churn.
  4. Serve immediately or ripe in freezer before serving.
