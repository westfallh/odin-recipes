Iced Coffee

Description

Learn how to make iced coffee with this iced coffee recipe! We use instant coffee in this cheater-version of iced cappuccino that's very easy to make, and very nice to drink!

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoon warm water
  • 2 teaspoons instant coffee
  • 1 cup ice
  • 6 fl oz milk

If necessary

  • 1 teaspoon sugar

Steps

  1. Combine water, coffee and sugar in a sealable jar. Shake until foam
  2. Pour into glass with ice, add milk
